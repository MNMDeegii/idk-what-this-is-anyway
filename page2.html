Sarcosuchus

Sarcosuchus is the ancestor of modern salt water crocodile. It sometimes dubbed "super-croc" Its 12.2M long, and weight 8 tons Large predator that even T-Rex were used to avoid from them(It was also T-Rex's nightmare).
